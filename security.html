Zero Trust Architecture Axioms

SAFENET adheres to three core axioms in all internal infrastructure and client deployments:

  1. Never Trust, Always Verify: Every byte of input is treated as a potential carrier for a payload or algorithmic manipulation.
  2. Least Privilege Access: Information is processed in isolated silos before being aggregated by the scoring systems.
  3. Assume Breach: The system actively scans for indicator patterns of compromise within seemingly "legitimate" communication channels.

System Resilience & Performance

Our infrastructure deploys advanced mitigation to ensure uninterrupted threat detection:

  • Scan Deduplication: Hash-based LRU Cache for redundant payload analysis.
  • Parallel Forensics: Async subprocess execution for internal engines simultaneously testing domains in FFT and ELA.
  • Circuit Breakers: Built-in safeguards that maintain client-side protection even if ML Enrichment APIs disconnect.
