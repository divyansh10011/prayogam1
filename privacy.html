Privacy Policy

Our Privacy-First Detection Policy and data handling procedures.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Privacy-First Detection Principle

SAFENET was designed with a core commitment to user privacy. We implement local forensic extraction at the edge (via our WASM modules and ztas-ai-widget.js) to minimize data exfiltration. Threat detection happens primarily in your browser and on your infrastructure.

2. Data Collection and Usage

When you use the SAFENET ecosystem, we collect telemetry only necessary for threat intelligence:

  • Cryptographic hashes of suspicious URLs and binary payloads.
  • Anonymized spectral audio coefficients (MFCCs) – we do not store raw voice recordings.
  • Aggregated metadata from the Triple-Lock security pipeline.

3. The ML Enrichment Layer

Data passed to our heavy-duty FastAPI backend (safe.py) is transient. Vision Transformers (ViT) process imagery in memory, and Error Level Analysis (ELA) is conducted without writing the raw files to disk unless explicitly opted into a forensic investigation.

4. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to request access to or deletion of your organizational threat intelligence data connected to your SAFENET account. Contact our privacy team for immediate compliance tracking.
